Ice Soup

Home

Description

A delicious dessert to end the day right!

Ingredients

  • 2L filtered Water
  • 1Kg of Ice Cubes

Steps

  1. Get a large bowl
  2. Pour 500Ml Water in the bowl, and add 250 grams of Ice Cubes
  3. Get a Spoon and mix for 45 seconds
  4. Repeat steps 2 & 3, 4 more times
  5. Enjoy
